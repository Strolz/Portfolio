Anton Strolz

I’m Anton, a front‑end developer who loves turning ideas into smooth, intentional interfaces. What started as curiosity has grown into a focused practice built around clarity, structure, and the craft of making digital experiences feel effortless. My background in IT taught me how systems connect behind the scenes, but front‑end development is where everything clicked. I’m drawn to the part of the web where logic meets design — where a single interaction can change how someone feels about a product. That intersection of usability, visual rhythm, and clean architecture is where I do my best work. I build projects that teach me something: dynamic interfaces, DOM‑driven state logic, responsive layouts, and small tools that sharpen my understanding of JavaScript. Every project in my portfolio reflects a progression milestone — not just what I made, but what I learned while making it. I value intentional practice, thoughtful design decisions, and writing code that feels clear, readable, and human. Outside of the code itself, I’m motivated by growth. I document my process, refine my workflow, and treat each project as a step toward becoming a stronger, more deliberate developer. My goal is simple: create interfaces that feel intuitive, build systems that make sense, and keep improving through consistent, meaningful practice.
